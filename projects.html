midPoint

midPoint is an app that makes it easier to plan meetups. Simply input the addresses or locations from where each person is coming from, along with the type of place you want to visit, and midPoint will find the venue closest to the middle. midPoint was an app initially conceived at CodeDay NJ Winter 2015 by Rishi Masand and me. We made an iOS app in 24 hours at CodeDay, and decided to continue working on the app afterwards. midPoint is available on Android and iOS. Check out the code here!
